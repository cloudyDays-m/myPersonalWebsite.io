More About Me

When I’m not reading about machine learning or working on a project, I like to slow down and recharge. I’ve been playing piano for a few years now, and it’s one of my favorite ways to unwind. I also really enjoy reading—mostly a mix of fantasy and thought-provoking classics. Some books that have stuck with me are Meditations by Marcus Aurelius, The Stranger by Albert Camus, and pretty much anything by Dostoevsky.

I’m also a fan of quiet nights, rainy days, and avid fan of tea (chamomile is my favorite).
